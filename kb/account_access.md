# Account Access & KYC (Fintech SaaS)

## Login issues
- Password reset email may take up to 5 minutes.
- Ask the customer to verify: email address, spam folder, and whether SSO is enabled.

## 2FA
- If the customer lost 2FA device, follow the recovery process:
  1) Verify identity via registered email + last invoice ID (or last 4 digits of card).
  2) If enterprise: request admin confirmation.
  3) Reset 2FA only after verification.

## KYC
- KYC verification can take up to 24 hours.
- If KYC fails: request updated documents and ensure names match exactly.

## Account suspension
- Suspensions may occur due to compliance triggers (e.g., AML flags) and require Security escalation.
