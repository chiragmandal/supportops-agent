# Billing & Subscription Policy (Fintech SaaS)

## Invoices
- Invoices are available in the dashboard: Settings -> Billing -> Invoices.
- Invoices include VAT where applicable. VAT ID can be updated in Billing settings.

## Refunds
- Monthly plans: refundable within 14 days of first purchase if usage is below 3 active days.
- Annual plans: refundable within 30 days of purchase; prorated refunds are not offered.
- Add-ons (e.g., extra users, AML checks): non-refundable once consumed.

## Chargebacks
- If a customer initiates a chargeback, account access may be limited until resolved.
- Support must escalate chargebacks to the Billing queue.

## Subscription changes
- Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
